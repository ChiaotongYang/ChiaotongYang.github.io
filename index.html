Zhanpeng Zhou (周展鹏)

I am an undergraduate student majored in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UMJI-SJTU.

My research interests range across explainable AI, graph representation learning and game theory. I used to be a research assistant at OMNILab, SJTU, under the supervision of Prof. Yaohui Jin. After that, I joined John Hopcroft Center, SJTU, under the supervision of Prof. Quanshi Zhang. I am also responsible for several research projects at WangLab, UMJI-SJTU, tutored by Prof. Xudong Wang. Recently, I am working as a summer intern at Mila under the supervision of Prof. Jian Tang.

I will pursue my Ph.D degree under the supervision Prof. Quanshi Zhang in Shanghai Jiao Tong University after gradutation.
