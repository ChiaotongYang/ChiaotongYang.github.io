Zhanpeng Zhou (周展鹏)

I am an undergraduate student majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UM-SJTU JI.

My research interests range across explainable AI, graph representation learning and game theory. Now, I am an undergraduate researcher at John Hopcroft Center, SJTU, studying interpretable AI under the supervision of Prof. Quanshi Zhang. Before that, from May. 2019 to Aug. 2020, I used to be a research assistant at OMNILab, SJTU, studying knowledge graph under the supervision of Prof. Yaohui Jin. From Sep. 2020 to Jun. 2021, I participated in two research projects and was responsible for leading one project on edge computing at WangLab, UMJI-SJTU, tutored by Prof. Xudong Wang. From Mar. 2021 to Jun. 2021, I was working on molecule property prediction and knowledge graph reasoning at Mila, HEC, under the supervision of Prof. Jian Tang.

I will pursue my Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Prof. Quanshi Zhang at Shanghai Jiao Tong University after graduation.
